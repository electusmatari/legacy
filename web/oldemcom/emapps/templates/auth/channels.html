Channels

Electus Matari members have access to a number of channels. This page lists most of them and offers you to join them with a simple press of a button.

Operations - EM
Main combat channel, shared with our allies. It's a very good idea to join for anyone, as war target intel is posted here as well.

Other Channels

These channels can be useful, but if you do not want to join them, that is up to you.

Orientation - EM
A channel set up especially for new members of the alliance and new pod pilots. You are welcome and encouraged to ask any and all questions in this channel. We have experienced pilots there who want to answer them for you.
Public - EM
The alliance's public diplomatic channel. If you're in here, you'd best be tactful, as the people here are those we are looking to pursue useful relations with.
Meetings - EM
Sometimes, a group in EM wants to do a meeting to discuss a certain topic. This channel is then used. Please do not hang around it while you are not participating in any meeting.

Intel Channels

Most regions have more or less public intel channels to warn the inhabitants of hostile movements. Electus Matari maintains two of them, and has access to a few others.

Molden Intel
Public intel channel for Molden Heath. Good to be in if you travel on Molden Heath low-sec.
Republic Intel
Public intel channel for Heimatar and Metropolis. Good to be in while travelling through the low-sec areas of these regions.
IntelOfGW
Intel channel of Great Wildlands, maintained by Republic Alliance.
True Intel
Intel channel of Great Wildlands, maintained by True Reign.

Restricted Access Channels

These are the very secret channels of Electus Matari. Due to security concerns, not everyone has access.

Capitals - EM
A channel for capital pilots, used primarily during operations. See this post for details on how to get access.
Commanders - EM
Fleet Commander discussion channel. Op preparations, some intel. Access is given once you have regularly run combat operations.
Directors - EM
Discussion channel for the directors and council members of the alliance.
