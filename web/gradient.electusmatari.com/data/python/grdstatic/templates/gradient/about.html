Gradient Corporation

Gradient is a corporation loyal to the Minmatar Republic. As such, we obey and uphold its laws, policies, and international treaties, and work for its continued and increased safety, prosperity, and freedom. We support the Republic in both war and peace. We fight her wars, but never forget that as much as fighters need ships, citizens and refugees need homes, and the Republic still needs to be strong once the war is over.

We have extensive industrial experience and produce ships and modules of all sizes and tech levels. Pilots, corporations and alliances that support the Republic can use our industrial backbone using our shop—see the Shop link in the menu.

In addition, we do have experienced combat pilots and fleet commanders, and we regularly patrol Republic space for criminals and other hostiles.

We are recruiting pilots loyal to the Republic. See the Recruitment link in the menu.

Gradient is a roleplaying corporation in the fictional universe of EVE Online.
